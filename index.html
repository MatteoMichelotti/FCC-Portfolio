About me:

My name is Matteo, I am originally from Italy but I now live in the US. I have a Master's degree in Physics, and I'm a self taught web developer.

My skill set includes: Node.js, Express.js, JavaScript, jQuery, MongoDB, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Semantic UI, Git, GitHub, but I'm always working to learn more! At the moment I'm learning a different approach to databases, this time using relational DB with SQL and sqlite.

After graduating with a Master of Science in Physics in 2014, my passion moved toward web development. I have recently completed several online education programs, focusing my time at Udemy.com and FreeCodeCamp, and I continue to develop my skills and languages.

I've come a long way since my earliest programming studies, when, at the age of 15, I took classes in computer architecture and programming, which taught me basic languages such as C, Pascal and Assembly. At university, I continued to improve my skills with a different set of more specialized languages, like R, Matlab, Python and Fortran.

Over the course of my education and experience, exploring different coding languages and environments gave me a solid grasp on the fundamentals of programming, of the structure rather than the syntax, allowing me to quickly pick up new ones when needed.
